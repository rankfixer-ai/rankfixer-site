About RankFixer

Managed Operations, Built for Reliability

RankFixer provides facility-based Filipino virtual assistants from a 200-seat TESDA-accredited training center in Bislig City, Philippines. We solve the root cause of outsourcing failure: infrastructure.

Why We Exist

Most Outsourcing Fails Because of Infrastructure, Not Talent

Freelancers working from home face power outages, unreliable internet, zero oversight, and constant distractions. Businesses lose weeks of productivity to ghosting, moonlighting, and downtime.

RankFixer was built to solve this. We moved the workforce into a professionally managed facility with redundant internet, backup power, and on-site supervision. The result: VAs who show up every day, work under structured management, and deliver consistent output.

TESDA-Accredited Training

Pre-Trained and Productive from Day One

As a TESDA-accredited training center, we pre-train every VA on AI tools, automation workflows, and professional communication before they touch client work.

TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) is the Philippine government agency that sets and certifies training standards. Accreditation means our training programs meet government-verified quality benchmarks — not self-assessed claims.

By the time a RankFixer VA starts on your account, they are already proficient in ChatGPT, Claude, Zapier, project management tools, and professional communication. You don't spend weeks waiting for someone to get up to speed.
