How Much Does a Filipino Virtual Assistant Cost in 2026?

A complete pricing guide covering hourly rates, monthly packages, managed vs freelance costs, and annual savings compared to US hiring.

Filipino VA Pricing Overview

Filipino VAs cost between $600 and $2,500+ per month depending on the hiring model, role type, and what's included. The headline rate tells you very little — the real question is what you're actually buying.

Hourly Rates: What to Expect in 2026

  • Entry-level VAs: $3–$5/hour (admin, data entry, basic tasks)
  • Mid-level specialists: $5–$8/hour (social media, customer support, content)
  • Senior/executive VAs: $8–$15/hour (project management, EA, specialist roles)

These are typical freelance platform rates. Managed providers bundle hourly cost into a monthly rate that also includes infrastructure, overhead, and management.
