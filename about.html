About RankFixer

RankFixer was started after watching too many agencies charge $5,000/month for work that could be done by a $12/hour Filipino VA.

We're not an agency. We don't manage campaigns or take a cut of your budget. We just publish honest, practical guides on hiring Filipino virtual assistants for SEO work.

Why This Site Exists

Most content about "hiring VAs" is either:

  • Written by agencies trying to sell you their services
  • Generic advice that doesn't address SEO-specific skills
  • Outdated information about rates and platforms

We wanted a resource that tells you:

  • Exactly what to pay (not "it depends")
  • Which tasks are worth delegating
  • Red flags to avoid when hiring
  • How to onboard someone in 48 hours

Our Approach

We believe in:

  • Transparency: Real rates, real examples, no fluff
  • Fair pay: Filipino VAs deserve competitive wages, not exploitation
  • Practical advice: Everything we publish is tested and actionable

Partner Platform

We partner with Job Copilot PH, a free job aggregation platform with 3,162+ active VA profiles. They don't charge placement fees or take a cut — just connecting businesses with Filipino talent.
